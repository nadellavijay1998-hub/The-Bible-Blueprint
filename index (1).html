About Vijay

Welcome to The Bible Blueprint, a ministry dedicated to exploring Scripture with depth, clarity, and practical application. With a background in both technology and theology, I blend analytical thinking with spiritual insight to help modern Christians navigate their faith.

My mission is to make Biblical truth accessible, engaging, and transformative. Whether through podcast episodes, YouTube teachings, or written articles, I aim to equip believers with a deeper understanding of God's Word.
